About Us

Welcome to, "What Was The Reason!!!", A Murder Mystery Podcast!

Who are we?

We are three girls who are invested in murder cases and would like to share it with the world. We wanna invite everyone to discover underrated cases that have not been solved due to multiple reason.

In 2024 we discovered that all three of us are into true crime and wanted to start a podcast to discuss cases but also share them with others (you guys!) So have a look and maybe we'll make more than one season! ;)
